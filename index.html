Instructions for building a self-destructing bicycle

Preparation

First, somehow get the following ingredients:

  • A hose
  • A funnel
  • Various skrewdrivers
  • A bicyle

Process

Then repeat the following steps until the bicyle destructs itself:

  1. Attach the funnel to one end of the hose
  2. Keep the other end shut with your thumb
  3. Fill one skrewdriver into the funnel
  4. Place the other end of the hose at your mouth
  5. Rapidly lift the funnel-end into the air while keeping your throat open
  6. Drive your biclye around the block

Conclusion

Be careful not to damage the hose.
